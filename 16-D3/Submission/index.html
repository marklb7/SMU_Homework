Correlations Discovered Between Percent of Population Lacking Healthcare and Percent of Population in Poverty

Based on our data, there seems to be a strong positive correlation between the percentage of people who are in poverty with the percentage of people who lack healthcare.

One can also observe that the states with a higher percentage of their population living in poverty are concentrated in the South.

This data is based on US Census Bureau 2014 ACS 1-year estimates.
